I am a postgraduate researcher in Political Science, ranked in the top 5% at Bangalore University, with a specialization in geopolitical risk, South Asian affairs, and open-source intelligence. My work sits at the intersection of academic rigor and policy relevance.

I produce structured intelligence briefs, risk snapshots, and policy articles that translate complex regional dynamics into clear, evidence-based assessments. I have a foundational OSINT certification and apply systematic source evaluation to monitor early political signals and emerging threats.
